The Top 5 Best Restaurants in the San Diego Airport

By The Author
June 3, 2023

If you’re looking for a quick bite before your flight or a sit-down meal to while away a layover, San Diego International Airport has plenty of dining options to suit your needs. From fast food to fine dining, there’s something for everyone at SDIA. Here are five of the best restaurants at San Diego International Airport.

#1- The Habit Burger Grill

The Habit Burger Grill is a California-based chain that specializes in made-to-order burgers and sandwiches. The Habit has been voted “Best Tasting Burger in America” by Consumer Reports and is a favorite among burger lovers. If you’re looking for a quick, delicious meal at SDIA, The Habit is a great option.

#2- Stone Brewery

Stone Brewery World Bistro & Gardens is a full-service restaurant located in Terminal 2 West. Serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner, Stone Brewery offers a menu of American classics with a twist. With an extensive beer list featuring local and international craft brews, Stone Brewery is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a meal before your flight.

#3- C Level

C Level Lounge is an upscale lounge located in Terminal 2 West that offers stunning views of the airport runway. C Level Lounge is open to all passengers and features a full bar and menu of small plates. Whether you’re looking to unwind with a cocktail or grab a quick bite, C Level Lounge is worth a visit.

#4- In & Out Burger

In-N-Out Burger is one of California’s most iconic fast food chains, and luckily for travelers, there’s an In-N-Out location inside SDIA. If you’re craving a double-double or animal style fries, In-N-Out is the place to go. Just be warned that the lines can get long during peak travel times!

#5- ShakesBurgerBeer

ShakesBurgersBeer is an airport favorite thanks to its tasty burgers and handcrafted shakes. Located in Terminal 2 East, ShakesBurgersBeer serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with an emphasis on fresh ingredients and made-to-order items. If you’re looking for a hearty meal before your flight, ShakesBurgersBeer should be your go-to spot.

San Diego International Airport has no shortage of dining options to suit every need and taste bud. Whether you’re looking for fast food or fine dining, there’s something for everyone at SDIA. The next time you find yourself at the airport, be sure to check out one (or all!) of these five wonderful restaurants.
