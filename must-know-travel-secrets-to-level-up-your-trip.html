Must-know Travel Secrets To Level Up Your Trip

By The Author
June 3, 2023

Whether you want to level up your in-flight experience, pick the best seat on the plane, or simply decide the best time to book a flight, life above 35,000 feet is filled with quandaries. Fortunately, it does not take much to find the answers. All you need is a little insider information to discover top travel secrets to make your flight more fun and smooth-sailing.

Subscribe to an airline’s newsletters

One of the biggest traveling expenses is airfare costs, especially if you are taking a trip abroad. If you wish to avoid skimping on your meal budget or your souvenirs, one way to save money is by purchasing discounted airplane tickets. You can easily nab discounts by subscribing to newsletters. Airlines typically offer promos and deals through email. Simply put your name on their lists so you can be updated on the promo codes, vouchers, and other special offers.

Be careful not to catch a cold

One research study shows that low cabin humidity and close quarters lower immunity and make individuals in the room 113 times more susceptible to autoimmune diseases than on a typical day. So make sure to bring your own water, take vitamin C, keep your nose moisturized, and sanitize your hands when in the air. If you are near a sick passenger, ask the flight attendant if it’s possible to switch seats. 

Don’t always book multiple tickets at once

If you are planning to purchase multiple tickets, search for them individually. The price of plane tickets varies depending on the travel agency; many times, individual seats are significantly cheaper than a block. Also, if you search for, say, five tickets, and they only have four at the lowest fare, all five are bumped to a more expensive price bracket. If you decide to purchase individual tickets, make sure that there are no per-ticket processing fees that would offset the savings. 

See what your airplane looks like before boarding.

Don’t blindly select your airplane seat. Before booking a flight, simply type the airline and flight number you’re interested in into sites like SeatGuru, Kayak, SeatMeastro, or other alternatives. By using these website, you can view seat maps, the exact lay out of your plane, and customer reviews of airplane seats. They will let you know which seat has extra legroom, which is the closest to the lavatory, where to find power jacks, and other relevant information.

Getting unexpected freebies is possible if you ask.

While airlines do not bother advertising it, there are certain freebies and above-and-beyond services that you can get if you ask. A mini bottle of drinking water, a full can of soda, or extra snacks can usually be easily acquired from the flight attendants, while small unexpected perks like a band-aid or sanitizing wipes may require a special request but are typically on hand for the flight crew. If the flight is delayed, many airlines also offer free premium drinks or snacks.
