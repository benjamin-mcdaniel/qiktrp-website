What Are The Best Music Festivals To Attend

By The Author
June 3, 2023

Attending music festivals is a great method to escape from your regular day-to-day routine and recharge your energy and spirit. In this article, we explore the hottest music festivals in the world that we’re certain you’ll enjoy. 

Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival

Coachella is the most attended, highest-grossing, and hottest music festival on the planet. No other music fest has as much impact on the music industry as this grand music event. Held in a sun-drenched grassy polo complex in Indio, California, Coachella is both a lively celebration of South Californian spirit and a platform for musicians to share a moment with a huge crowd. 

If you are planning to head off to this annual music and festival, make sure to bring your favorite sunscreen spray, sunglasses, hands-free bag, easy-pitching tents, portable chargers, and other essentials to make the most out of the experience. 

Tomorrowland Music Festival

Tomorrowland is a popular music festival held in Boom, Flanders, Belgium. This long-running event is often celebrated as the biggest dance music party in the world. Held on an imaginative space called Tomorrow Holy Grounds, attendees consider Tomorrowland the most imagination-defying and mystical experience a person can have without leaving the planet. The fest includes artists across EDM, techno, trance, drum n bass, and house spread over ten mindblowing stages. 

Built around wild storytelling and mythical imageries, Tomorrowland is a must-experience for dance music fans. 

Glastonbury Festival

No other music fest has done more to develop, nurture, and celebrate the peculiarities of modern music fest culture quite like the people of Glastonbury. This five-day occasion, known colloquially as Glasto, takes place in Pilton, Somerset, England. It has served as a ground zero for many counter-cultural movements in the 70s and 80s, and is one of the only few music festivals to take “fallow years,” mostly at five-year intervals to give the land a break. 

If you are planning to visit England, don’t miss out on Glasto. The festival has been through various incarnations over the years, but it remains to be an iconic event that promotes tolerance and acceptance among a diverse variety of music. 

Electric Daisy Carnival

EDC is an annual dance music fest held every summer in different parts of Las Vegas, Orlando, China, Mexico, and other countries across the globe. This event takes over the world every May featuring the ultimate spectacle of lighting, sound, and the most prominent performers in techno, house, drum n bass, and other genres of music. 

If your searching for a unique multi-day music fest that incorporates carnival themes and cutting edge stage production, and sets the standards  in the live entertainment industry, EDC is one of the music festivals you should consider attending.
